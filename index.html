Advice For Accommedations

In this episode of Concise Advice, Lexi Diaz explores how students access accommodations in college—from learning and housing support to mental health resources. With guest Connor Hoch, she sheds light on the real experiences behind the paperwork, helping students understand their options and feel more confident in seeking support.

Navigating Between Worlds
